Stationers’ Register Online contains the entries for the literary, musical and artistic works made in the Registers of the Stationers’ Company of London between 1557 and 1640. Originally derived from Edward Arber’s edition of the Register, this release has corrected Arber against the original manuscript sources while retaining his notes and comments. Future releases will extend the period of coverage and add standardised names for persons and works. For all queries, contact the editors at editors@stationersregister.online
