Stationers’ Register Online presents the copy-entries for literary, musical and artistic works listed in the Registers of the Stationers’ Company of London. It is jointly derived from the original manuscript sources and from Edward Arber’s transcription, with additional commentary. As of 2017 (SRO) includes ‘copies’ for the first three registers, which record entries from 1557 until 1620. Liber D (up to 1640) is in progress.
